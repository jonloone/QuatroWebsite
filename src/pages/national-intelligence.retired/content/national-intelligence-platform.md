Meanwhile, adversaries operate across all domains simultaneously. Cartels use maritime routes, land borders, financial systems, and digital recruitment pipelines as a single integrated operation. They see one battlefield. Your agencies see seven separate screens.

Quatro eliminates the gap. One platform connects every sensor, every agency, and every data source into a single operational picture — with predictive intelligence, cross-domain correlation, and multi-agency coordination from a unified command environment.

## Three Intelligence Programs

Each program addresses a critical national security domain. Each is built on the same unified platform. Each connects through a single operational picture.

### Youth Vulnerability Index

Predictive intelligence that identifies which communities and which youth are most at risk of organized crime recruitment — before it happens. Multi-agency data fusion, parish-level scoring, targeted intervention.

The Youth Vulnerability Index fuses data from police, education, health, and social services into a single predictive intelligence system — scoring parish-level vulnerability weekly and enabling targeted intervention against organized crime recruitment networks. Five tiered intelligence products — from daily operational summaries to deep-dive threat assessments — create an ISR capability that transforms fragmented data into a predictive system.

### Counter-Cartel Operations

Cross-domain intelligence fusion for counter-narcotics, criminal network mapping, financial crimes, and coordinated multi-agency enforcement operations.

Transnational criminal organizations operate across maritime routes, land borders, financial systems, digital communications, and urban territories as a single integrated operation. Counter-cartel intelligence fuses five operational domains — maritime, border, financial, network, and urban — into one targeting picture. When a dark vessel is detected off the coast, the platform automatically correlates it with known trafficking routes, recent financial activity in port cities, border crossing patterns, and network intelligence on the organization that operates the corridor.

### Resource Protection

Satellite, ground sensor, and AI-powered detection of illegal mining, pipeline theft, deforestation, and environmental destruction — with automated enforcement targeting.

Illegal mining operations fund the same criminal networks that traffic drugs, launder money, and recruit youth. Resource protection intelligence fuses satellite earth observation, pipeline SCADA, environmental IoT sensors, and maritime vessel tracking into persistent surveillance that detects new extraction activity within hours — not months. Evidence-grade audit trails support the full chain from detection event to courtroom prosecution.

## Seven Security Domains

Quatro provides operational intelligence across the full spectrum of national security priorities, each domain powered by the same unified platform.

### 01 — Critical Infrastructure Protection

Real-time monitoring of energy grids, water systems, transportation networks, and telecommunications. AI-powered anomaly detection identifies sabotage, equipment degradation, and cascading failure risks. Automated incident response workflows achieve sub-30-second detection-to-response capability.

### 02 — Border Security

Multi-modal sensor integration — ground sensors (fiber optic, pressure, vibration), CCTV/infrared at critical crossing points, and autonomous drone patrols. Pattern analysis identifies smuggling routes and unauthorized crossings. Automated alerts dispatch rapid-response units to precise GPS coordinates.

### 03 — Maritime Sovereignty

AIS/VMS vessel tracking, dark vessel detection, and multi-source intelligence fusion across the exclusive economic zone and protected marine reserves. Real-time tracking of 1,000+ vessels with dark vessel identification. Route prediction and enforcement targeting for illegal fishing and narco-trafficking.

### 04 — Drug Interdiction

Border camera networks, pattern analysis of trafficking routes, and interdiction planning tools. Threat network intelligence maps criminal organizations and predicts movement patterns. Network disruption recommendations guide coordinated enforcement operations.

### 05 — Natural Resources & Mining

Pipeline vibration detection and pressure analysis for theft identification. Satellite monitoring of illegal mining operations with automated change detection. Environmental compliance monitoring across extraction sites. Evidence-grade detection for regulatory and law enforcement handoff.

### 06 — Financial Crimes

Pattern analysis across financial transaction data. Criminal network mapping and link analysis. Predictive intelligence for money laundering and illicit financial flows connected to organized crime. Correlation with operational intelligence across all other domains.

### 07 — Environmental Protection

Vessel tracking and route prediction for illegal fishing enforcement. Satellite-based deforestation and habitat monitoring. Real-time environmental sensor networks for air and water quality across protected areas and marine reserves.

### Command — Operational Response

All feeding directly into operational decision-making and coordinated response.
