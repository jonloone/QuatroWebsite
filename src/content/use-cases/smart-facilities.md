## Overview

Modern facilities integrate dozens of interconnected systems -- HVAC, electrical distribution, water management, fire suppression, security, elevators, and lighting -- that are connected by design but managed in isolation. Quatro unifies every building system into a single intelligent operational model, enabling cross-system optimization that cuts energy costs by 40%, guarantees 99.2% uptime, and reduces maintenance spend by 30%.

For venues hosting events, the platform adds crowd flow management, dynamic environmental coordination, and multi-system safety orchestration -- reducing event-day energy spikes by 60% and post-event recovery time by 75%. The building transforms from a drag on operations into a strategic asset that generates value through efficiency, reliability, and occupant experience.

## Key Outcomes

- **Energy consumption** reduced from 48,200 kWh to 28,900 kWh per month
- **System uptime** improved from 98.1% to 99.2%
- **Maintenance costs** reduced from $156K to $109K annually
- **Emergency service calls** cut from 32 to 8 per year
- **Peak load** reduced by 18%, lowering utility demand charges
- **Occupant comfort score** improved from 6.2 to 8.7 out of 10
- **Event-day energy spikes** reduced from +32% to +12% above baseline
