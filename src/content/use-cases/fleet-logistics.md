## Overview

Fleet operations generate massive volumes of telematics data that remain trapped in disconnected systems -- engine health in one platform, fuel consumption in another, GPS tracking in a third. Quatro unifies every data source across the fleet into a single operational model, enabling predictive maintenance that prevents breakdowns, real-time route optimization that cuts fuel costs, and depot coordination that maximizes throughput.

The platform transforms fleet management from reactive crisis handling to continuous optimization. By correlating vehicle health, driver behavior, route conditions, and depot capacity in real-time, Quatro delivers $4.2M in annual fuel savings, 40% improvement in on-time delivery, and 74% fewer in-field breakdowns -- turning a cost center into a competitive advantage.

## Key Outcomes

- **Annual fuel spend** reduced from $11.2M to $7.0M
- **On-time delivery rate** improved from 71% to 99%
- **Maintenance costs** cut from $8.6M to $6.5M annually
- **Vehicle downtime** reduced from 8.2% to 2.1%
- **Safety incidents** reduced by 58% per 1000 vehicle-miles
- **Depot utilization** improved from 67% to 91%
- **CO2 emissions** reduced by 26% annually
