## Overview

Modern telecommunications networks span 5G radio access, legacy fiber, microwave backhaul, satellite connectivity, and cloud systems -- creating a complexity that overwhelms traditional monitoring approaches. Quatro unifies every data source across the network stack into a single operational model, applying AI-driven correlation to eliminate false alarms, predict fault propagation, and deliver root cause analysis in seconds.

The platform transforms NOC operations from reactive firefighting to strategic infrastructure management. By understanding the true dependencies between hardware, protocols, and services across multi-vendor environments, Quatro ensures that operators see only actionable events tied to business impact -- enabling 55% faster incident resolution and 99.8% service availability.

- **False alarm rate** reduced from 85% to 5% of daily alerts
- **Mean time to resolution** cut from 4.2 hours to 1.9 hours
- **Service availability** improved from 99.1% to 99.8%
- **Unplanned maintenance events** reduced by 35% per quarter
- **Spectrum utilization** improved from 68% to 82%
