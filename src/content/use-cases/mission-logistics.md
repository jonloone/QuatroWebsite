## How It Works

Mission Logistics & Force Readiness unifies supply chain visibility across geographically dispersed forward operating bases. Instead of waiting hours for stock reports and coordination calls, commanders see real-time inventory levels, in-transit shipments, and personnel readiness across their entire area of operations.

### Multi-FOB Supply Fusion
Inventory data, requisition queues, transport schedules, and personnel status flow from each FOB into Quatro's event processing layer. The system correlates demand (which bases need what, when) with supply (what's in stock, what's in transit, transit time estimates).

### Intelligent Demand Prediction
Machine learning forecasts logistics needs before crisis occurs:
- Predict part failures before equipment breaks down
- Anticipate supply depletion before stockouts occur
- Model transport delays and proactively reroute shipments
- Alert commanders to readiness risks days in advance

### Automated Fulfillment Workflows (Governed Autonomous Action)
When a supply need is detected:
- System automatically searches inventory across all FOBs
- Prioritizes fulfillment based on commander-defined rules and mission urgency
- Coordinates transport with available assets and transit constraints
- Routes personnel through staging areas on optimal deployment paths
- Generates real-time status updates so commanders know ETA within minutes

### Federated Architecture for Operational Security
Each FOB maintains local compute and local data. Quatro synchronizes logistics intelligence across bases without centralizing sensitive operational details—preserving classification levels and command authority.
