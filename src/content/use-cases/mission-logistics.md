## Overview

Military mission success depends on getting the right equipment, supplies, and personnel to the right place at the right time. Quatro unifies inventory management, maintenance records, transport tracking, and personnel systems into an end-to-end logistics operational picture with predictive maintenance, automated demand forecasting, and dynamic personnel matching -- enabling 55% faster logistics response and 42% faster emergency deployment.

## Who This Is For

- **Logistics Command Centers** overseeing supply chain, inventory, and transport for multi-base operations requiring end-to-end visibility and predictive demand insights.
- **Forward Operating Base Commanders** managing equipment readiness, supply levels, and personnel allocation under operational constraints.
- **Maintenance & Asset Managers** responsible for vehicle, aircraft, and equipment readiness across the fleet, seeking to shift from reactive to predictive maintenance.
- **Demand Planning & Supply Chain Analysts** coordinating supply ordering, inventory positioning, and transport scheduling across depots and bases.
