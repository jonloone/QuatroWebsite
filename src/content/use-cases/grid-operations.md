## Overview

Power grids are transforming as solar and wind generation shift from supplemental resources to dominant sources. This transition demands a fundamental change in how grids are managed: from assumptions of stable, predictable supply to real-time orchestration of intermittent generation, storage, and flexible demand.

Quatro unifies every element of the grid -- generation plants, renewable inverters, battery storage, distribution substations, demand response programs, and weather feeds -- into a single operational model with real-time state visibility at every node. Operators transition from reacting to outages to anticipating imbalances 30-60 minutes in advance, enabling proactive resource activation instead of crisis response.

The platform's multi-layer demand forecasting, automated economic dispatch, and renewable smoothing capabilities ensure grid stability even at 40%+ renewable penetration, while continuous NERC event logging eliminates the manual compliance overhead that consumes hundreds of hours annually.

## Key Outcomes

- **Grid restoration time** reduced from 45-60 minutes to 15-20 minutes
- **Unplanned outage frequency** cut from 8-12 to 5-7 per year
- **Renewable curtailment rate** reduced from 8-12% to 2-4%
- **Demand forecast accuracy** improved from 88-92% to 95%+
- **Frequency deviation incidents** reduced from 6-10 to 1-2 per month
- **NERC compliance audit time** cut from 120+ hours to 40 hours per year
- **Emergency generation costs** reduced from $2-4M to $400K-800K annually
