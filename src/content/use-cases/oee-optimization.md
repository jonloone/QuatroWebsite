## Overview

Production line OEE optimization with Quatro delivers real-time visibility into every stage of discrete manufacturing operations. By connecting PLCs, sensors, and MES systems into a unified data layer, the platform automatically calculates OEE at both line and product level, pinpoints bottlenecks, and predicts quality issues before they halt output.

For production managers, plant quality engineers, and manufacturing operations leaders, the platform replaces reactive firefighting with proactive management. Changeover optimization draws on historical performance data to recommend fastest sequences, while predictive maintenance flags equipment degradation before breakdowns occur.

Across multi-site operations, Quatro benchmarks performance and recommends production allocation strategies, ensuring best practices from top-performing lines become the baseline for all facilities.
