## Overview

Military installations face an evolving spectrum of physical and insider threats that demand faster, more coordinated responses than traditional fragmented security systems can deliver. Quatro unifies CCTV, radar, thermal sensors, access control, and perimeter detection into a single operational picture with automated threat correlation and response -- achieving sub-30-second detection-to-response times in fully air-gapped environments.

## Who This Is For

- **Installation Commanders** responsible for force protection and operational security across bases housing personnel, classified assets, or critical infrastructure.
- **Base Security Officers** managing CCTV, access control, perimeter sensors, and guard schedules across multiple disconnected systems.
- **Insider Threat Program Managers** tasked with detecting behavioral anomalies and potential sabotage indicators before incidents occur.
