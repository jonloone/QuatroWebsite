## Overview

Naval and coast guard operations demand total maritime domain awareness across territorial waters and Exclusive Economic Zones extending hundreds of miles offshore. Quatro fuses AIS, radar, satellite imagery, SIGINT, and HUMINT into a unified ocean picture that detects dark vessels in minutes, correlates vessel identity across sources, and enables real-time intelligence sharing with coalition partners through governed, classification-aware channels.

## Who This Is For

- **Naval Command Centers** overseeing territorial waters and strategic maritime zones, requiring real-time decisioning for vessel interdiction and coalition coordination.
- **Coast Guard Operations** responsible for EEZ enforcement, illegal fishing interdiction, smuggling suppression, and SAR coordination across constrained patrol resources.
- **Intelligence Fusion Analysts** correlating multi-source maritime intelligence for real-time and strategic assessments.
- **Coalition & Interagency Commanders** coordinating multi-nation maritime operations and intelligence sharing.
