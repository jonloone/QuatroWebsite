## How It Works

Maritime Domain Awareness requires seeing beyond what traditional systems reveal. Quatro fuses AIS broadcasts, radar sweeps, satellite imagery, and signals intelligence into a unified operational picture—and then applies machine learning to detect anomalies.

### Multi-Sensor Fusion Engine
All maritime data streams converge into Quatro's event processing layer. The utility correlates vessel movements, identifies dark ships (those operating without transponders), and flags suspicious behavior patterns—all in real-time.

### Intelligent Vessel Classification
Every contact is automatically risk-scored based on:
- Vessel type, flag, and historical behavior
- Speed, course changes, and route patterns
- Transponder status and signal anomalies
- Known associations with illegal activity

### Actionable Command Workflows
When a threat or illegal activity is detected:
- Commander receives real-time alert with target position, classification, and engagement recommendation
- Automated approval gates enforce rules of engagement
- Dispatch systems route naval assets to intercept coordinates
- Secure audit trail documents every action for compliance and accountability

### EEZ-Scale Monitoring
Monitor your entire exclusive economic zone without network fragmentation. Deploy edge compute nodes on coastal installations and naval vessels; command staff see correlated intelligence from a single dashboard.
