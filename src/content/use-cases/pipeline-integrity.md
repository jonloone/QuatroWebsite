## Overview

Pipeline integrity is both an operational metric and an environmental imperative. Midstream operators managing hundreds or thousands of miles of transmission infrastructure face a persistent gap between the data they collect and the actionable intelligence they need. SCADA systems, fiber optic distributed temperature sensing, satellite imagery, and manual field inspections each generate valuable signals -- but when these data streams operate in silos, critical correlations are missed and response times suffer.

Quatro bridges that gap by unifying every data source into a single operational backbone, applying real-time anomaly correlation and predictive analytics to detect leaks in hours instead of days. The SMARTs Edge Gateway deploys at remote field locations, pre-filtering anomalies in harsh environments before transmitting upstream, while the Intelligence Platform correlates pressure signatures, thermal data, and historical patterns to deliver high-confidence alerts with minimal false positives.

## Key Outcomes

- **Leak detection time** reduced from 8-12 days to 2-4 hours
- **False alarm rate** cut from 45+ to 25 per month
- **Planned vs. emergency maintenance** ratio improved from 30/70 to 70/30
- **Compliance audit preparation** reduced from 200+ hours to 80 hours per year
- **Mean time to repair** cut from 6-8 hours to 3-4 hours
- **Product loss from leaks** reduced from 2-3% to under 0.5% annually
