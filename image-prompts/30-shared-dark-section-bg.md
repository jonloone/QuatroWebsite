## REFRESH OF EXISTING SHARED BACKGROUND

## Output Target
**Filename:** `bg.jpg`

## Description
A minimal, abstract dark background designed to serve as a section background for text overlay. This is NOT a detailed photographic scene — it is a carefully composed gradient and texture composition that works at any crop or aspect ratio.

---

## Scene Composition
A deep navy-to-black gradient background (navy at top left, fading to near-black at bottom right) with extremely subtle textural overlays:
- Very faint topographic or data-grid texture pattern suggesting geography and data
- Extremely subtle node-and-line network pattern suggesting connectivity and systems
- The patterns should barely be visible, more felt than seen — they provide visual interest without demanding attention

The entire composition is intentionally minimal and serves purely as a background. It must be dark enough to work behind white text with high contrast, yet visually interesting enough to avoid feeling flat or boring.

---

## Composition Notes
- **Aspect Ratio Flexibility:** Must work at any crop/aspect ratio — no dominant focal point
- **Edge Behavior:** Gradient and texture should feel seamless if tiled or cropped
- **Foreground Safe:** Entire image must work as background with prominent text/content overlay
- **Subtlety:** Texture and patterns are extremely subtle — never distracting
- **Vertical and Horizontal Safe:** Safe areas on all sides for various text placements

---

## Color & Mood Notes
- **Primary Palette:** Deep navy (#1a2332 range) at top; black (#0a0e17 range) at bottom
- **Accent Suggestions:** Extremely subtle hints of Quatro forest green (#0f4c3a) in the network lines or grid, nearly imperceptible
- **Lighting:** No obvious light source; evenly lit with subtle gradient emphasis
- **Mood:** Professional, technical, atmospheric, data-driven, sophisticated
- **Treatment:** Clean and minimal; desaturated; no visible photographic elements
- **Atmosphere:** The invisible infrastructure; subtle suggestion of systems and connectivity

---

## Technical Specifications
- **Resolution:** 1920 x 1080px (landscape) — but must function at any aspect ratio
- **File Size:** <500KB
- **Content Restrictions:** Completely abstract; no faces, logos, text, UI elements, or identifiable objects
- **Style:** Abstract gradient with subtle texture overlay; NOT photographic
- **Texture Pattern:** Topographic lines and node-network patterns at <5% opacity
- **NOT:** Busy, detailed, photographic, or demanding visual attention
