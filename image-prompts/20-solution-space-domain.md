## Scene Description
A space surveillance telescope or radar installation perched at a remote, elevated location under a star-filled night sky. The facility's white dome structure is partially open, revealing the massive optical or radar equipment inside. The viewpoint captures the vast emptiness above contrasted with precision instrumentation below. Light trails from orbiting satellites streak across the sky — long-exposure effect showing the tracks of objects in space. The Milky Way visible but not dominant; stars crisp and clear. The technical equipment precisely mounted and maintained. A sense of the bridge between Earth and the void above.
