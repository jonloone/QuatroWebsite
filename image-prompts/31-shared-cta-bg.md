# Shared Background: Call-to-Action Section
## REFRESH OF EXISTING SHARED CTA BACKGROUND

---

## Output Target
**Filename:** `cta.jpg`

---

## Description
A minimal, abstract dark background specifically designed for call-to-action sections where white text with dark overlay will be prominently displayed. This background is intentionally even more minimal than the general dark section background — it provides visual interest while remaining completely recessive, letting the CTA message command full attention.

---

## Scene Composition
A subtle gradient background (deep navy-to-black, slightly warmer than the standard dark section) with extremely faint textural elements:
- Deep navy/black base with hints of dark forest teal and very subtle deep forest green undertones
- A gentle light source from the upper-left creating a subtle gradient toward that corner
- Extremely faint texture of geographic contour lines or grid pattern — barely visible, almost subliminal
- Minimal visual noise; the emphasis is entirely on supporting text readability and message clarity

This background is designed to sit behind a 75% dark overlay containing white CTA text. The background itself should be unobtrusive and professional.

---

## Composition Notes
- **Aspect Ratio Flexibility:** Must work at any crop/aspect ratio — completely non-directional
- **Edge Behavior:** Seamless; gradient and texture work at any crop or aspect ratio
- **Foreground Safe:** Entire image works as background with prominent white text over dark overlay
- **Text Support:** Designed to support high-contrast white text with background dark overlay
- **Minimalism:** Even more minimal than the standard dark section background
- **Light Direction:** Subtle light source from upper-left creating gentle directional gradient

---

## Color & Mood Notes
- **Primary Palette:** Deep navy (#1a2332 range) with hints of dark teal (#0f3a3a range) and dark forest green (#0a2a1f range)
- **Warmth:** Slightly warmer than pure navy-to-black; hints of deep teal/forest tones suggest connection to Quatro brand
- **Lighting:** Gentle light source from upper-left; warm accent light creating subtle gradient
- **Accent Colors:** Quatro forest green (#0f4c3a) present only as a deep undertone, nearly invisible
- **Mood:** Professional, premium, action-oriented, trustworthy, sophisticated
- **Treatment:** Extremely clean and minimal; desaturated; no photographic or busy elements
- **Atmosphere:** A refined, focused environment for the CTA message

---

## Technical Specifications
- **Resolution:** 1920 x 1080px (landscape) — but must function at any aspect ratio
- **File Size:** <500KB
- **Content Restrictions:** Completely abstract; no faces, logos, text, UI elements, or identifiable objects
- **Style:** Abstract gradient with minimal texture overlay; NOT photographic
- **Texture Pattern:** Contour lines and grid at <3% opacity (even less visible than dark section bg)
- **Overlay Consideration:** Designed to work behind a 75% dark overlay with white text on top
- **NOT:** Busy, detailed, photographic, or competing for visual attention
