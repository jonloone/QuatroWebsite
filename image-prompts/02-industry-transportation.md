## Scene Description

A major container port photographed at dawn, featuring:
- Massive container ship occupying the center-right of composition, being loaded by gantry cranes
- Multiple gantry cranes active and positioned at dramatic angles—these should be prominent structural elements
- Stacked shipping containers in orderly rows on the dock, extending into depth and across the frame
- Morning mist rising from the water surface and settling in the industrial landscape
- Warm amber and golden light breaking through clouds on the horizon, illuminating metal surfaces and water
- A cargo aircraft visible in the distant sky, reinforcing multi-modal logistics
- Industrial waterfront infrastructure: berths, loading equipment, power lines, dock machinery
- Ground-level or mid-level perspective showing both the scale of operations and intricate movement of goods
- Sense of constant, coordinated global logistics movement and scale
