## Scene Description
A bustling container port captured from an elevated vantage point, showing the orchestrated complexity of modern port logistics. Multiple cargo ships at berth with containers stacked high on deck. Massive gantry cranes mid-operation, moving containers between ship and dock. A queue of semi-trucks waiting to load or unload. Rail infrastructure visible with boxcars waiting. Hundreds of colorful containers stacked in precise rows. The entire scene is a study in coordinated movement — vehicles, cranes, ships, and people all moving in choreographed flow. Late afternoon light creates long shadows and texture across the stacked cargo.
