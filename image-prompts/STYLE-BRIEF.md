# Quatro Image Generation — Style Brief

Apply these rules to EVERY image prompt in this folder system.

## Brand Alignment

- **Brand:** Quatro — operational intelligence for physical infrastructure
- **Promise:** "Action for Tomorrow"
- **Positioning:** "The Humanized Edge" — bridge between raw industrial data and mission-critical decisions
- **Tone:** Authoritative, visionary, technically precise

## Color Direction

Match the Quatro "Light-Industrial" palette:

- **Forest Green** `#0f4c3a` — brand primary
- **Cyber Emerald** `#00D1B2` — accent (data states, highlights)
- **Intelligence Navy** `#0A1128` — deep trust sections
- **Rich Charcoal** `#1A1A1A` — dark tones
- **Safety Amber** `#F4A261` — warm accent, operational lighting

Images should lean into deep teals, forest greens, navy blues, and warm ambers. Avoid saturated primaries (red, bright blue, neon green).

## Visual Tone

- Cinematic, atmospheric, slightly desaturated
- Editorial/documentary — NOT stock photography
- Professional, operational, high-stakes
- "Clean Energy" aesthetic — sophisticated, generous space
- Infrastructure at scale, operations in progress

## Hard Rules

- No text, logos, or UI elements baked in
- No visible human faces (privacy)
- No pure black backgrounds — use charcoal/navy
- Equipment, environments, abstract operational contexts only
- Optimized for web: <500KB after compression

## Three Image Tiers

| Tier | Use | Dimensions | Composition |
|------|-----|-----------|-------------|
| **Hero** | Page background behind white text + 60% dark overlay | 1920×1080 | Subject right, text space left |
| **Capability** | Bento card image, dark card bg below | 1200×750 | Centered, specific/close-up |
| **Proof** | Editorial split, light bg, rounded shadow | 1200×750 | Centered, documentary/evidentiary |

## Industry Pillar Visual Direction

Each pillar has a distinct visual territory while staying within the brand palette:

| Pillar | Primary Mood | Dominant Colors | Subject Matter |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Energy** | Industrial scale, operational | Amber + Charcoal | Pipelines, substations, grid infrastructure, control rooms |
| **National Security** | Authority, precision, classified | Navy + Emerald | Air-gapped environments, command centers, perimeters, sovereign facilities |
| **Networks** | Connectivity, convergence, signal | Emerald + Navy | Satellite dishes, antenna arrays, signal chains, teleport facilities |
| **Critical Infrastructure** | Civic, essential, built environment | Forest Green + Amber | Urban systems, water treatment, ports, transportation corridors |
| **Finance** | Trust, governance, intelligence | Navy + Amber | Modern banking architecture, abstract data convergence, compliance structures |

Finance images lean toward abstract data-art and architectural photography rather than literal banking imagery (no coin stacks, stock tickers, or currency). The visual metaphor is *systems becoming unified* — convergence, resolution, audit trails.
