# Quatro Homepage Rework — Strategy & Copy Direction

**Version 1.0 — March 2026**
**Status:** Draft for alignment before implementation

---

## Guiding Principles

This rework is grounded in four decisions made during the homepage audit:

1. **The human is the hero.** Quatro is the lightsaber, not the Jedi. Every section positions the platform as what enables the human to do extraordinary things — not as the thing that matters most.
2. **"Systems That Think. People Who Build."** This is the new hero line. It's in the lineage of IBM's "Machines Should Work. People Should Think." — same structural logic, completely different words and meaning.
3. **No cliché language.** No "empowering," "enabling," "powering," "unlocking," "complexity," "handle." No urgency manufacturing. No PAS temporal narratives.
4. **The homepage is a routing page.** It doesn't try to sell every buyer on everything. It gives each buyer type a fast path to recognize themselves and go deeper into the industry pages.

### Voice for this page

Light-Industrial. Precise, confident, civic-minded. Empathetic framing — names structural conditions, not failures. Calm confidence — assumes Quatro is the obvious choice once the buyer understands what it does. No exclamation points.
